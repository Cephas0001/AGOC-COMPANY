About Godfred

Godfred Ampomah is a visionary entrepreneur from Kumasi, Ghana, and the founder of Ampomah Group of Companies. He is also the co-founder of SEMDU Real Estates and Lands, where he contributes to innovative real estate solutions that create lasting value.

Born and raised in Kumasi and now based in the United States, Godfred is committed to building businesses that foster sustainable growth and bridge opportunities between Africa and the world.

With a diverse portfolio spanning real estate, travel, delivery services, and automotive solutions, Godfred brings a unique perspective to business development and cross-continental collaboration.
